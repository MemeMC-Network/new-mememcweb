Server Rules

Please read and follow all rules to ensure a fun experience for everyone

General Rules

No Griefing

Do not destroy or modify other players' builds without permission. This includes stealing items from chests.

Be Respectful

Treat all players with respect. No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination of any kind.

No Cheating

Use of hacked clients, exploits, or any unfair advantages is strictly prohibited.

Chat Rules

No Spamming

Avoid excessive messages in chat, command spamming, or flooding.

English Only

Please keep all public chat in English so staff can moderate effectively.

No Advertising

Do not advertise other servers, websites, or services without permission.

Gameplay Rules

No PvP Abuse

Do not repeatedly kill players who don't want to PvP. Respect PvP zones and rules.

No AFK Farms

Automated farms that run while AFK are not allowed. Semi-automatic farms are permitted.

Follow Staff

Always comply with staff instructions and decisions. If you disagree, discuss it privately.

Consequences

Breaking these rules may result in:

  • Temporary mute or chat restriction
  • Temporary ban from the server
  • Permanent ban for severe/repeated offenses
  • Rollback of unauthorized builds/actions

Staff reserve the right to take appropriate action for any behavior that disrupts the server, even if not explicitly listed here.
